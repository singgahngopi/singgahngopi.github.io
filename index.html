what's make our coffee special!

Welcome to Singgah Ngopi, your go-to coffee shop located at SHELL Chalok Kedai. Since 2022, we have been serving a delightful selection of specialty coffees and mouthwatering pastries. At Singgah Ngopi, we believe in the art of brewing and take pride in offering a range of coffee options to suit every taste. Pair your favorite brew with our delectable pastries, baked fresh daily, for a truly satisfying experience. Visit Singgah Ngopi and let us elevate your coffee and pastry indulgence to new heights.
